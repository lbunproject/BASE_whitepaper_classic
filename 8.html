The U.S. Supreme Court formulated what came to be called the "Howey test" (a set of four factors). If all four factors are present in a given instrument, it's an investment contract (and thus a security). The four factors are: 1. An investment of money, 2. In a common enterprise, 3. With a reasonable expectation of profit, 4.
